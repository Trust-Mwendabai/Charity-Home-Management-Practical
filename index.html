🍰 Eggless Cake Recipe

Traditional Method with Modern Convenience
Eggless Vanilla Cake
⏱️ Total: 50 mins
A moist and fluffy traditional cake made without eggs, using locally available ingredients. Can be made with or without an oven using the steaming method - perfect for homes without modern appliances.
🛒 Ingredients
  • Plain flour (unga wosakaniza) - 1½ cups
  • Baking powder - 1 teaspoon
  • Baking soda - ½ teaspoon (optional)
  • Sugar - ¾ cup
  • Cooking oil - ½ cup
  • Fresh milk or water - 1 cup
  • Lemon juice or vinegar - 1 tablespoon
  • Salt - pinch
  • Vanilla essence - 1 teaspoon (optional)
  • Optional: mashed banana - 1 medium
👩‍🍳 Cooking Methods
🔥 Steaming Method (No Oven):
• Place batter in greased tin
• Steam in large sufuria for 40-50 minutes
• Keep on low to medium heat
• Check with toothpick before removing

🔥 Baking Method:
• Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F)
• Bake for 30-35 minutes
• Test doneness with toothpick
• Cool before removing from tin

👥 Serves: 6-8 people
💰 Cost: Budget-friendly

🥤 Fresh Lemonade Recipe

Perfect Companion Drink
Fresh Homemade Lemonade
⏱️ Prep: 10 mins
A perfectly balanced homemade lemonade using fresh lemons. Quick to prepare, naturally refreshing, and the ideal companion to cake. Rich in Vitamin C and perfect for hot weather.
🍋 Ingredients
  • Fresh lemons - 2 large (or 3 medium)
  • Sugar - ¼ to ½ cup (adjustable to taste)
  • Cold clean water - 3 cups
  • Salt - pinch (enhances flavor, optional)
  • Ice cubes - as needed
  • Fresh mint leaves - for garnish (optional)
⚡ Quick Steps
Step 1: Roll lemons on counter to soften
Step 2: Cut and extract fresh juice (strain seeds)
Step 3: Mix lemon juice with sugar until dissolved
