🍰 Eggless Cake Recipe

Traditional Method with Modern Convenience
Eggless Vanilla Cake
Total: 50 mins
A moist and fluffy traditional cake made without eggs, using locally available ingredients. Can be made with or without an oven using the steaming method.
🛒 Ingredients
  • Plain flour (unga wosakaniza) - 1½ cups
  • Baking powder - 1 teaspoon
  • Baking soda - ½ teaspoon (optional)
  • Sugar - ¾ cup
  • Cooking oil - ½ cup
  • Fresh milk or water - 1 cup
  • Lemon juice or vinegar - 1 tablespoon
  • Salt - pinch
  • Optional: mashed banana - 1 medium
👩‍🍳 Cooking Methods
Steaming Method:
• No oven needed
• Steam in sufuria for 40-50 minutes
• Keep on low heat

Baking Method:
• Oven at 180°C
• Bake for 30-35 minutes
• Test with toothpick

Serves: 6-8 people

🥤 Fresh Lemonade Recipe

Perfect Companion Drink
Fresh Homemade Lemonade
Prep: 10 mins
A perfectly balanced homemade lemonade using fresh lemons. Quick to prepare and naturally refreshing - the ideal companion to cake.
🍋 Ingredients
  • Fresh lemons - 2 large (or 3 medium)
  • Sugar - ¼ to ½ cup (adjustable)
  • Cold clean water - 3 cups
  • Salt - pinch (optional)
  • Ice cubes - as needed
⚡ Quick Steps
Step 1: Roll lemons to soften
Step 2: Extract fresh juice
Step 3: Mix with sugar until dissolved
Step 4: Add cold water and stir
Step 5: Taste and adjust sweetness
Step 6: Serve with ice

Serves: 3-4 cups
